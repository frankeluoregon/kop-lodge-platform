## Food Service

### Buffet
- 1 buffet line per 75 guests
- Queue space: 5 sq ft/person
- Approach clearance: 4'
- Behind buffet: 2' (restocking)
- Total buffet zone depth: 12'
- Serving time: ~1 min/guest

### Food Stations
- Station footprint: 48-80 sq ft each
- 1 station per 40-50 guests
- Space between stations: 6'
- Action station (chef-attended): 100 sq ft min

### Plated Service
- 1 server per 20 guests
- Kitchen access aisle: 48"
- Behind seated guests trail: 36"

## Bar / Beverage

- Standard bar section: 8'L x 2'D x 42"H
- Back bar depth: 3'
- Total footprint: 6' deep, 48 sq ft
- Queue zone: 8' deep x 10' wide
- Total bar zone: ~128 sq ft
- 1 bartender per 75 guests
- 1 bar section per 100 guests
- Distribute bars 30'+ apart to prevent bottlenecks
- Beer/wine only: faster service, 1 server per 100 guests
- Distance from bar to nearest table: 5' min

## Kids Area

- 35 sq ft per child (NAEYC standard)
- 200 sq ft minimum
- Toddlers: 50 sq ft per child
- Fence height: 36" (24" for toddler)
- Staff ratios: 1:4 infants, 1:6 toddlers, 1:8 preschool, 1:12 school-age
- Keep 30' from bars, 50' from stage sound
- Near restrooms and parent seating

## ADA Accessibility

- Path width: 36" min, 60" for two-way
- Turning radius: 60"
- Doorway: 32" min clear, 36" ideal
- 5% of seats accessible (min 1-6 depending on capacity)
- Table: 28-34" height, 27" knee clearance, 30" wide, 19" deep
- Ramp: 1:12 max slope, 36" wide, 30" max rise per run
- Assistive listening devices required for assemblies of 50+

## Fire Code

### Occupancy Limits
- Assembly (standing): 5 sq ft/person
- Assembly (chairs, unfixed): 7 sq ft/person
- Assembly (unconcentrated): 15 sq ft/person

### Exits
- 2 exits required at 50 occupants
- 3 exits at 500, 4 at 1000
- Exit width: 0.2" per occupant, 36" minimum
- Doors swing outward for 50+ occupancy
- Max travel to exit: 200' (unsprinklered), 250' (sprinklered)
- Exit signs illuminated, emergency lighting required

### Aisles
- Main: 44" min
- Side: 36" min
- Dead end max: 20'

## Flow Optimization

- **Circulation**: allocate 20-30% of total area
- **Primary paths**: 8' wide, secondary: 5'
- **Never narrow below 4'**
- **Natural flow**: guests tend clockwise
- **Zoning**: loud→quiet gradient, bar near entrance, food away from dance floor, kids at periphery, vendors along perimeter, anchor attractions at corners
- **Queue**: 5 sq ft/person, max 10 min acceptable wait
- **Restrooms**: 1 per 50 guests, accessible from all zones, max 200' walk
- **Parking**: 1 spot per 3 guests
- **Trash**: 1 can per 50 guests
